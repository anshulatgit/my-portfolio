# Getting Started with AI

Artificial Intelligence is the defining skill of our generation. Whether you're a student or professional, starting your AI journey today will shape your entire career.

## Why Learn AI?

The demand for AI-skilled professionals is growing rapidly. With millions of AI-related job openings globally, the opportunity has never been bigger — especially for Indian students who can compete globally.

## Step 1: Build Your Foundation

Before deep learning, you need solid foundations in Mathematics, Python, and Data basics.

## Step 2: Learn Machine Learning

Start with classical ML before jumping to deep learning — Linear Regression, Decision Trees, and Clustering.

## Step 3: Build Projects

Theory alone won't land you jobs. Build real projects using Kaggle datasets and HuggingFace models.

The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is right now.
