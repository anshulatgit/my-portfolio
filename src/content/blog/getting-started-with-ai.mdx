# Getting Started with AI: A Beginner's Complete Roadmap

Artificial Intelligence is the defining skill of our generation. Whether you're a student, professional, or curious learner, starting your AI journey today will shape your entire career.

## Why Learn AI?

The demand for AI-skilled professionals is growing at **40% year-over-year**. With over 1 million AI-related job openings globally, the opportunity has never been bigger — especially for Indian students who can compete globally from day one.

## Step 1: Build Your Foundation

Before deep learning, you need solid foundations:

- **Mathematics**: Linear Algebra, Calculus, Statistics
- **Python**: The universal language of AI
- **Data Basics**: How to work with datasets

## Step 2: Learn Machine Learning

Start with classical ML before jumping to deep learning:

1. Linear & Logistic Regression
2. Decision Trees & Random Forests
3. Support Vector Machines
4. Clustering & Dimensionality Reduction

**Best Resource**: Andrew Ng's Machine Learning course on Coursera (free to audit)

## Step 3: Deep Learning

Once you understand ML:

1. Neural Networks basics
2. CNNs (for images)
3. RNNs & LSTMs (for sequences)
4. Transformers & Attention (the backbone of modern AI)

## Step 4: Build Projects

Theory alone won't land you jobs. Build real projects:

- Image classifier using your own dataset
- Sentiment analysis tool
- Chatbot with LangChain
- Recommendation system

## Step 5: Specialize

Choose your path:

- **Computer Vision** → self-driving cars, medical imaging
- **NLP** → chatbots, translation, summarization
- **Reinforcement Learning** → gaming, robotics
- **MLOps** → deploying AI in production

## Resources to Start Today

- Fast.ai (practical, top-down approach)
- DeepLearning.AI specialization
- Kaggle (competitions + free notebooks)
- HuggingFace (state-of-the-art models)

The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is right now.
